What is PAN CARD?

PAN card is a GOV ID acts as legal identity verification and photo for various purposes, including obtaining a gas connection,opening a bank account,loan etc in INDIA.

About this Prototype

This feature is designed and developed to keep the data secure as per the current situation of online loan fraud and cyber Crime.

Today,India is moving like a bullet train in every sectors specially digital india which makes our life easy and comfortable by introducing UPI -unified Payments interface but at the same time we don't forget that online frauds & cyber crime has also increased due to digitization.So,in the era of digitization we must keep our eyes open and alert when we upload personal & sensitive data to any unknown app for loan or verification. As i realized that we must make high security app where our PAN or adhaar details will be registered and if anyone try to verify our documents with or without our consent,then user will get request from particular bank or person for verification.For verification respective company or bank needs User approval depends on his/her action. Even user can deactive or activate his PANCARD temporary if he don't required for certain time period.

Users can check their Pancard history of active or closed loans, pancard correction etc. in history tab section. We can add many features to avoid online frauds.
